# Code of Conduct

## Our Standards

chiron is a small, open-source project, and we expect everyone involved to treat each other with basic respect.

**Encouraged:**

- Being welcoming and patient with newcomers
- Giving and receiving constructive feedback gracefully
- Focusing on what is best for the project and its users
- Acknowledging when you don't know something

**Not acceptable:**

- Harassment, insults, or derogatory comments
- Personal or political attacks
- Public or private harassment of any kind
- Publishing others' private information without explicit permission
- Other conduct that would be inappropriate in a professional setting

## Enforcement

If you observe behavior that violates these standards, please contact the maintainer at **adhaithaam@gmail.com**. All reports will be reviewed promptly and fairly. The maintainer may take any action deemed appropriate, including warning, temporary ban, or permanent ban from the project.
